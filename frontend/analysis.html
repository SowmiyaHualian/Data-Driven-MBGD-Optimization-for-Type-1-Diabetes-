    About This Assessment

    This tool uses machine learning to assess Type-1 Diabetes risk based on clinical indicators and symptoms. The assessment is based on:

    • Clinical Indicators: Age, BMI, glucose levels, HbA1c
    • Symptoms: Physical manifestations of metabolic changes
    • Data Source: ICMR clinical datasets and research
    • Accuracy: Machine learning model trained on thousands of cases

    ⚠️ Disclaimer: This is not a medical diagnosis. Please consult a qualified healthcare provider for professional medical advice and diagnosis.
